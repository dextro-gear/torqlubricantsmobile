Speciality Lubricants for

Iron & Steel

The iron and steel industry has very volatile working environments. From extremely heavy loads to very high speed, from low temperatures to high temperatures, from dusty polluted environment to humid acidic environment­—the equipment is subject to various operating parameters. Therefore, with the right lubricant, you can extend the life of your machine and avoid premature failures and maintenance. Torq’s technical experts will advise you personally on what products will be the best choice for your application. In addition to the guidance, we are also open to creating specialty lubricants based on your specific requirements.
