Speciality Lubricants for

Sugar Industry

Torq’s specialty lubricants have been specially engineered to meet the needs of the heavily loaded mill roll bearings present in sugar mills. Torq oils are developed with high viscosity lubricating oil base stocks and are strengthened with the addition of carefully chosen non-toxic mild EP and special compounding agents. They display outstanding resistance to leakage and safeguard the bearings against rust and wear. Our expertise in developing specialty lubricants that are reliable, efficient and cost effective make us reliable lubrication partners for our clients in the sugar industry.
