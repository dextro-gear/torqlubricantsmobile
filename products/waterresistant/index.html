Water-Resistant High Performance Greases

A critical function of any grease is to participate effectively in the sealing of the system. Water ingress due to vapours, emulsions, jets under pressure or baths for cooling installations, etc. is a commonplace occurrence in many applications. While traditional greases are not generally soluble in water but persistent water ingress can cause substantial decline in the performance of the grease. Torq’s specialty Water-Resistant Greases are high performance water-repellent, hydrophobic grease formulations that are able to deliver quality lubrication even in the presence of high percentages of water.
