Open Gear Greases

TORQ has the full range of specialty Open Gear Greases developed specifically for applications where a viscous structure and adhesive nature is required. Offering enhanced adhesiveness for heavy duty applications, Torq’s open gear grease contains adequate solid lubricants and provides superior protection to heavy load and moisture exposed enclosed gears, heavy loaded transmission systems and slow moving open gears.
